What is Movie Fantasy League?

Movie Fantasy League (MFL) brings the excitement of fantasy sports to the world of cinema. Draft upcoming movies, track their real-world performance, and compete with friends to see whose film portfolio reigns supreme!


Whether you're a casual moviegoer or a dedicated cinephile, MFL offers a fun new way to engage with the films you love while testing your box office prediction skills.


How will you win your league? Remember, strategy is key! Do you go all-in on one blockbuster or build a diverse portfolio of films?


The choice is yours. Welcome to Movie Fantasy League!

League Setup

Create or Join a League

Start your own private league with friends or join a public league to compete with movie fans from around the world.


Each player gets a $100M budget to build their roster of upcoming films that will compete for points based on real-world performance.

Choose your League Format

  • Classic: Assemble your movies for the year within your $100M budget limit
  • Draft: Take turns selecting movies in a predetermined order
  • Prediction: Simply predict how much a film will make worldwide. Closer you are, the more points you gain!

Strategy is key! Do you go all-in on one blockbuster or build a diverse portfolio of films?

Movie Valuation

Movies are assigned values based on their anticipated performance, including projected box office, star power, and critical reception potential.

Scoring System

Box Office Performance

  • $1M in box office earnings = 1 point
  • Opening weekend bonus: Top 3 finish = 10 extra points

Critical Reception

  • Rotten Tomatoes Critic Score: 1% = 1 point
  • Rotten Tomatoes Audience Score: 1% = 1 point
  • "Certified Fresh" status = 20 bonus points

Awards & Recognition

  • Major Award Nomination = 5 points
  • Major Award Win = 10 points
  • Oscar Best Picture Win = 25 bonus points

Streaming Performance

  • Top 10 on major platform = 5 points per week
  • #1 position on major platform = 10 points per week

League Progression

Track your score on a live leaderboard as your movies are released throughout the year. Make strategic trades with other players to maximize your points potential.


Movies earn points as they're released, keeping the competition exciting all year long!

Endgame

The MFL season concludes at a predetermined date (such as Oscar night or the end of the calendar year). Before the season even begins, leagues can define their custom prizes based on final league positioning. No way out of this one. Looking at you, Ryan. Avenge your friends to avoid losing!


  • Cash prize pools
  • Trophy or memorabilia
  • Bragging rights
  • Custom league rewards
